Cookies

Find a legal aid adviser or family mediator puts small files (known as ‘cookies’) on your computer.

These cookies are used across the Find a legal aid adviser or family mediator website.

Find out how to manage cookies from the Information Commissioner's Office.

Essential cookies (strictly necessary)

We use an essential cookie to remember when you accept or reject cookies on our website.

Essential cookies we use
Name Purpose Expires
cookiePermission Saves your cookie consent settings 1 year

Analytics cookies (optional)

We use Google Analytics software to understand how people use Find a legal aid adviser or family mediator. We do this to help make sure the site is meeting the needs of its users and to help us make improvements.

We do not collect or store your personal information (for example your name or address) so this information cannot be used to identify who you are.

We do not allow Google to use or share our analytics data.

Google Analytics stores information about

  • the pages you visit
  • the categories you use to search
  • the postcode you use to search
  • how long you spend on each page
  • how you arrived at the service
  • what you click on while you visit the service
  • the device and browser you use
